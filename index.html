About me

UX/UI Designer Student

I'm a UX/UI Designer student based in Rome, Italy. I've always been passionate about many things which include travelling, reading, appreciating and practicing art in all of it's forms. My passion for creativity led me to open up to a complete different working prospective, that's why I decided to dive into UX/UI Design. I like to consider myself as a constant "work in progress", I learn new things everyday, I appreciate books, movies, trips and much more. I've always loved surrounding myself with inspiring contexts, it could be people, a certain place or even just a movie. The outcome of a great inspiration can be fulfilling at a deep level, not only professionally speaking but also personally. The biggest wish I have for myself is putting together the things that I love and getting to mold them and fit them into what could be my dream job.
